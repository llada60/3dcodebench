Each individual drupelet is a glossy, deep dark purple-black sphere with strong specular highlights giving a shiny, wet appearance. A short pale green stem protrudes from the top. Fine hair-like structures (fine drupelets) are visible around the edges between the main drupelet spheres. The deep shadowing between the drupelets creates strong surface contrast.
