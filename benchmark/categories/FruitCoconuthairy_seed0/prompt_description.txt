A hairy coconut rendered as a textured 3D model. The fruit is a perfectly round sphere entirely covered in a dense coat of long, fibrous hair strands rendered as individual thin curved fibers. The fiber coloring is warm golden tan with reddish-orange tints toward the upper hemisphere and lighter cream-yellow lower down, giving it a natural matted look. The dense fiber particle system creates a messy, tangled texture with no smooth surface visible beneath the hairs. This represents the inner fibrous husk (coir) of a mature coconut stripped of its outer green shell.
