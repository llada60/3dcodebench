A spiny lobster rendered as a textured 3D model. Unlike a true lobster, this crustacean lacks large front chelae (claws) and instead features two extremely long, stout, upward-curving antennae as its most prominent feature. The segmented abdomen is wide and composed of multiple smooth, rounded orange-red carapace segments. The cephalothorax is short and compact, with several pairs of small walking legs with pointed tips beneath. A small fan-shaped tail is visible at the rear. The smooth exoskeleton is a warm orange-red with slight specular highlights on the convex segment surfaces.
