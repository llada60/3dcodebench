To create the 3D object of a straight staircase using python blender code, one would model a series of rectangular step treads and risers as a single merged stair geometry extruded along a straight inclined path, add glass panel railing objects on left and right sides, connect them with tubular vertical posts and a horizontal handrail at the top, and apply a dark painted metal material to the structure with a translucent glass material to the balustrade panels.
